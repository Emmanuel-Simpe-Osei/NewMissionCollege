About NEW MISSION COLLEGE

New Mission College was established as a remedial institution in 2021 by the proprietor, Mr. Robert, with 65 students, comprising 34 boys and 31 girls. Since 2022, students have been enrolling and performing greatly in academics. Admission increases every year due to high academic excellence in WASSCE that the school receives from WAEC.

Modern Learning Environment

Our serene campus fosters focus, creativity, and collaboration among students.

Dedicated Teachers

Experienced tutors committed to nurturing academic excellence and discipline.

Academic Excellence

Consistent top performance in WASSCE, inspiring growth and confidence every year.

Vibrant Student Life

Beyond books, we inspire leadership, teamwork, and social responsibility.
